Tutorial

Step-by-Step Guide to Creating Your Story

Step 1: Complete the registration and log into your account. Once logged in, click the "Whispr" button in the navigation bar at the top of the page.

Step 2: After entering the story creation page, input your desired story title in the first field. This title will be used to generate a captivating cover image, so make sure to give your story a name!

Step 3: Next, enter the content of your story. You can either upload a voice MP3 file or type out your story in the provided text box. Once done, click the confirm button and wait for your story to be generated.

Step 4: After AI generation is complete, your content will be displayed as a series of images paired with text. You can freely adjust the order of the text by editing in the input field. Finally, press the "Create" button to complete the story creation process.

Step 5: Now, enjoy your newly created story! You can swipe left or right to view the entire story.
